## üéØ Purpose

This portfolio demonstrates practical GRC skills through real-world artifacts modeled after enterprise and SaaS security programs. It serves as a learning resource and professional showcase for cybersecurity risk assessment, compliance readiness, policy development, and audit evidence management.

**Context:** This is self-directed educational work designed to build and demonstrate GRC competency aligned with industry standards including ISO 27001, SOC 2, and NIST CSF.

## üìÇ Repository Structure

### **[0-About/](0-About/)**
Professional background, skills matrix, and resume links. Start here to learn about my GRC focus areas and career objectives.

### **[risk-register.md](risk-register.md)** | **[Risk-Register/](Risk-Register/)**
- **Primary artifact:** Comprehensive risk register with scoring methodology
- Includes sample risks, treatment plans, and heat map guidance
- **Folder:** Enhanced examples including third-party risk assessments
- **Framework alignment:** NIST CSF (Identify), ISO 27001 Clause 6.1.2

### **[soc2-readiness.md](soc2-readiness.md)**
- SOC 2 Type II readiness checklist
- Trust Services Criteria (TSC) gap assessment
- Control evidence tracking and remediation planning
- **Framework alignment:** AICPA Trust Services Criteria

### **[iso27001-toolkit/](iso27001-toolkit/)**
- Annex A controls mapping
- Statement of Applicability (SoA) templates
- ISMS scope and risk assessment methodology
- **Framework alignment:** ISO/IEC 27001:2022

### **[policies/](policies/)**
- Information security policies (acceptable use, incident response, change management, vendor risk)
- Designed for small-to-mid-size organizations
- **Framework alignment:** ISO 27001 Annex A.5, SOC 2 CC1

### **[audit-evidence-index.md](audit-evidence-index.md)**
- Centralized audit evidence tracker
- Links control areas to evidence locations
- Supports SOC 2 and ISO 27001 audit preparation
- **Framework alignment:** SOC 2 CC4 (Monitoring), ISO 27001 Clause 9.2
