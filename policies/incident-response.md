# Incident Response Policy

## Purpose
Define the requirements for detecting, reporting, and responding to security incidents.

## Scope
Applies to all systems, data, employees, and third parties handling organizational information.

## Roles & Responsibilities
- **Incident Response Lead:** Coordinates response and communications
- **IT/Security Team:** Triage, containment, eradication, recovery
- **Business Owners:** Support impact assessment and restoration
- **All Personnel:** Report suspected incidents immediately

## Policy Statements
1. Suspected incidents must be reported within 1 hour of discovery.
2. Incidents are classified by severity and tracked to closure.
3. Evidence must be preserved according to forensic best practices.
4. Communication plans must be followed for internal/external notifications.
5. Post-incident reviews must be completed within 10 business days.

## Incident Response Lifecycle
- **Preparation**
- **Identification**
- **Containment**
- **Eradication**
- **Recovery**
- **Lessons Learned**

## Review Cadence
Reviewed annually and after major incidents.
