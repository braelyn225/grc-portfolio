# Vendor Risk Management Policy

## Purpose
Establish requirements for assessing and managing third-party risk.

## Scope
Applies to all vendors and service providers handling company data or critical services.

## Roles & Responsibilities
- **Vendor Owner:** Initiates onboarding and ongoing monitoring
- **Security/Compliance:** Conducts risk assessments
- **Procurement/Legal:** Ensures contractual safeguards

## Policy Statements
1. Vendors must be risk-tiered before onboarding.
2. High-risk vendors require security assessments and contractual controls.
3. Data processing agreements must be in place where applicable.
4. Vendor performance and risk are reviewed at least annually.
5. Termination procedures must ensure data return or destruction.

## Review Cadence
Reviewed annually.
