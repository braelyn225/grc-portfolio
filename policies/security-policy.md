## Scope
Applies to all employees, contractors, systems, and data.

## Roles & Responsibilities
- **Executive Sponsor:** Approves the policy
- **Security Lead:** Maintains and enforces policy
- **All Personnel:** Comply with policy requirements

## Policy Statements
1. Access to systems and data is granted based on least privilege.
2. Multi-factor authentication is required for critical systems.
3. Security events must be reported within 24 hours.
4. Security controls are reviewed at least annually.

## Review Cadence
Reviewed annually or upon significant changes.
