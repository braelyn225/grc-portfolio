# Acceptable Use Policy

## Purpose
Set expectations for acceptable use of company systems and data.

## Scope
Applies to all users of organizational assets.

## Policy Statements
1. Systems are for authorized business use.
2. Users must not share credentials.
3. Users must not install unapproved software.
4. Data must be handled according to classification.

## Enforcement
Violations may result in disciplinary action.

## Review Cadence
Reviewed annually.
