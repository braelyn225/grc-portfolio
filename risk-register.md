# Risk Register

## Purpose
Document and track information security, compliance, and operational risks. This register supports risk treatment decisions and ongoing monitoring.

## Risk Scoring
- **Likelihood (L):** 1 (Rare) to 5 (Almost Certain)
- **Impact (I):** 1 (Negligible) to 5 (Severe)
- **Risk Score:** L × I
- **Risk Rating:**
  - 1–4 Low
  - 5–9 Medium
  - 10–16 High
  - 17–25 Critical

## Risk Treatment Options
- **Mitigate:** Reduce likelihood/impact via controls
- **Transfer:** Insurance, contracts
- **Accept:** Document and monitor
- **Avoid:** Stop the risky activity

## Review Cadence
- **Operational Risks:** Quarterly
- **Strategic Risks:** Semi-annually
- **Critical Risks:** Monthly
