### 1. **Risk Register & Risk Management Program**
**Location:** [risk-register.md](risk-register.md), [Risk-Register/](Risk-Register/)

**Objective:** Develop a comprehensive risk register to identify, assess, and track cybersecurity and operational risks.

**Framework Alignment:**
- **ISO 27001:** Clause 6.1.2 (Information security risk assessment)
- **NIST CSF:** Identify (ID.RA) - Risk Assessment
- **SOC 2:** CC3 (Risk assessment process)

**Deliverables:**
- Risk scoring methodology (Likelihood √ó Impact)
- 27+ identified risks with treatment plans
- Risk heat map visualization guide
- Third-party vendor risk assessment templates

**Skills Demonstrated:**
- Qualitative risk assessment
- Risk treatment decision-making (Mitigate, Transfer, Accept, Avoid)
- Business impact analysis
- Risk communication to stakeholders

**Outcome:** Created enterprise-grade risk register with clear ownership, mitigation timelines, and quarterly review cadence. Demonstrates ability to translate technical risks into business language for executive reporting.

### 2. **SOC 2 Readiness & Compliance Program**
**Location:** [soc2-readiness.md](soc2-readiness.md)

**Objective:** Prepare organization for SOC 2 Type II audit through gap assessment and control evidence collection.

**Framework Alignment:**
- **AICPA Trust Services Criteria:** All 5 categories (Security, Availability, Confidentiality, Processing Integrity, Privacy)
- **Common Criteria:** CC1-CC9 (Security baseline)

**Deliverables:**
- SOC 2 readiness checklist (9 TSC categories)
- Gap remediation plan with assigned owners
- Control evidence tracking matrix
- System boundary documentation

**Skills Demonstrated:**
- SOC 2 framework interpretation
- Gap analysis and remediation planning
- Control evidence collection
- Audit readiness preparation

**Outcome:** Structured approach to SOC 2 compliance covering all Trust Services Criteria. Includes actionable gap remediation with target dates, demonstrating project management and compliance program design.

### 3. **ISO 27001 ISMS Toolkit**
**Location:** [iso27001-toolkit/](iso27001-toolkit/)

**Objective:** Build Information Security Management System (ISMS) documentation to support ISO 27001 certification.

**Framework Alignment:**
- **ISO/IEC 27001:2022:** Full standard (Clauses 4-10, Annex A)
- **Annex A Controls:** 93 security controls across 4 themes

**Deliverables:**
- Annex A controls mapping to implemented controls
- Statement of Applicability (SoA) template
- ISMS scope definition
- Risk assessment methodology documentation

**Skills Demonstrated:**
- ISO 27001 standard interpretation
- Controls mapping and gap identification
- ISMS documentation and structure
- Security control implementation planning

**Outcome:** Comprehensive toolkit demonstrating understanding of ISO 27001 requirements. Controls mapped to evidence locations with clear ownership and implementation status.
