# Vendor Risk Scoring Model

## Purpose
This scoring model helps prioritize vendor risk based on the potential impact to the organization if the vendor were compromised or unavailable.

## Scoring Factors
Each vendor is scored on the following:

| Factor | Score Range |
|------|-------------|
| Data Sensitivity | 1–5 |
| System Access Level | 1–5 |
| Service Criticality | 1–5 |
| Regulatory Impact | 1–5 |

## Risk Calculation
## Risk Tiering
- **Low Risk:** 4–7
- **Medium Risk:** 8–14
- **High Risk:** 15–20

## Usage
High-risk vendors require:
- Enhanced due diligence
- Annual reassessments
- Contractual security obligations
- Review of independent audit reports (SOC 2)
