# Policies & Procedures

## Purpose
Security policies and procedures establish management intent and define how security and compliance requirements are implemented across the organization.

This folder contains sample policies commonly required for SOC 2, ISO 27001, and NIST-aligned environments.

## Policy Structure
Each policy follows a consistent, audit-friendly format:
- Purpose
- Scope
- Roles and Responsibilities
- Policy Statements
- Enforcement and Exceptions
- Review and Maintenance

## Included Policies
- Information Security Policy
- Access Control Policy
- Incident Response Plan
- Acceptable Use Policy
- Security Awareness Training Policy

These documents demonstrate documentation and governance skills expected of GRC analysts.
