# Incident Response Plan

## Purpose
This plan defines the process for identifying, responding to, and recovering from information security incidents.

## Scope
Applies to all security incidents involving company systems, data, or third parties.

## Incident Response Phases
1. **Preparation:** Policies, training, and tools in place
2. **Identification:** Detect and report potential incidents
3. **Containment:** Limit impact and prevent spread
4. **Eradication:** Remove root cause
5. **Recovery:** Restore systems and operations
6. **Lessons Learned:** Post-incident review and improvements

## Roles and Responsibilities
- **Incident Response Lead:** Coordinates response
- **IT/Security:** Technical investigation and remediation
- **Management:** Decision-making and communication

## Testing
Incident response procedures are tested at least annually.
