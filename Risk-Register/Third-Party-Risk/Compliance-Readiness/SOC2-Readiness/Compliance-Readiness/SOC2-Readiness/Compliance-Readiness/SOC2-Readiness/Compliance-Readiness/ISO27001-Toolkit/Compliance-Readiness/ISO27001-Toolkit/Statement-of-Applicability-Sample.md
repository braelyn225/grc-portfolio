# Statement of Applicability (SoA) – Sample

## Purpose
The Statement of Applicability (SoA) documents which ISO 27001 Annex A controls are applicable to the organization and provides justification for inclusion or exclusion.

## Sample Control Applicability

| Annex A Control | Control Description | Applicable | Justification |
|----------------|---------------------|------------|---------------|
| A.5.1 | Information security policies | Yes | Required to establish governance |
| A.6.1 | Access control | Yes | Protects systems and data |
| A.8.7 | Malware protection | Yes | Reduces risk of compromise |
| A.5.23 | Information security incident management | Yes | Required for detection and response |
| A.8.28 | Secure coding practices | No | Development outsourced |

## Notes
Controls marked “No” must include clear, defensible justification.
