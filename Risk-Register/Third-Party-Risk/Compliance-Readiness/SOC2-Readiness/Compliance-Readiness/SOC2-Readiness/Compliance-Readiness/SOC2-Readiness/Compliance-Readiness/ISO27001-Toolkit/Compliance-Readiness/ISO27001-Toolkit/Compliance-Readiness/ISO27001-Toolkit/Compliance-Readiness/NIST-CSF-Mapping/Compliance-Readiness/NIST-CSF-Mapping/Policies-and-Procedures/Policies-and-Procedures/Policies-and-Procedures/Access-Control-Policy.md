# Access Control Policy

## Purpose
This policy defines requirements for granting, managing, and revoking access to information systems and data.

## Scope
Applies to all systems, applications, and users.

## Policy Statements
- Access is granted based on the principle of least privilege.
- User access must be approved and documented.
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is required for privileged access.
- Access reviews are conducted at least quarterly.

## Responsibilities
- **IT/Security:** Provision and deprovision access
- **Managers:** Approve access requests
- **Users:** Protect authentication credentials

## Review
Reviewed annually or upon material changes.
