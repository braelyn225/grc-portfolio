# Acceptable Use Policy

## Purpose
This policy defines acceptable and prohibited use of company information systems.

## Scope
Applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties with system access.

## Acceptable Use
- Use systems for authorized business purposes
- Protect company information and credentials
- Report suspected security incidents promptly

## Prohibited Use
- Unauthorized access to systems or data
- Sharing credentials
- Installing unauthorized software
- Using systems for illegal activities

## Enforcement
Violations may result in disciplinary action.
