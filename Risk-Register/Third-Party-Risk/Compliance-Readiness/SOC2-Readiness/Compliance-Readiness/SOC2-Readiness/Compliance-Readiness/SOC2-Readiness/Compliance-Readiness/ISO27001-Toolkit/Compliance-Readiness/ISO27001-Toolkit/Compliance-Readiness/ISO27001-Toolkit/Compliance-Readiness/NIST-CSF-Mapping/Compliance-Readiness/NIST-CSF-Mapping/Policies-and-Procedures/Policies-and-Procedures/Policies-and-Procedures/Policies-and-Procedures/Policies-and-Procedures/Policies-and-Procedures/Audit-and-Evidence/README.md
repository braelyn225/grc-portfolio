# Audit & Evidence Management

## Purpose
Audit and evidence management ensures that security and compliance controls are operating effectively and can be demonstrated to internal and external stakeholders.

This folder contains templates and guidance commonly used during SOC 2, ISO 27001, and internal audits.

## Key Activities
- Evidence identification and collection
- Audit finding documentation
- Remediation tracking and corrective actions
- Ongoing compliance monitoring

## Artifacts Included
- Evidence Collection Playbook
- Audit Finding Template
- Corrective Action Plan (CAP) Template

These artifacts reflect real-world GRC audit support workflows.
